V-Bahn AG collaborates with global manufacturers of innovative products for railway infrastructure and supplies them to the accross Ukraine. Our partnerships with these recognized industry leaders allow us to offer advanced technologies and solutions to optimize railway transportation in Ukraine.

Among them:

Schwihag

Switzerland

Schwihag is a recognized leader in the production and supply of innovative solutions for railway infrastructure. They specialize in the manufacturing of fastening systems, rails, sleepers, and other components that ensure the safety and efficiency of train operations. Our collaboration with Schwihag allows us to utilize cutting-edge technologies to optimize railway transportation.

Ukrainian Railway

Ukraine

Ukrainian Railway is a key player in the development and maintenance of the railway system in Ukraine. Their experience and expertise assist us in implementing projects with high efficiency and precision, contributing to the development of the railway transport in the country.

GSI Group

Ukraine

GSI Group is a manufacturer of prestressed concrete sleepers and beams for use in railway infrastructure. The company has extensive experience in producing high-quality concrete structures that meet the requirements of modern standards and technologies.

Kyivguma

Ukraine

We also have the honor of partnering with Kyivguma, a leading manufacturer of rubber products and components in the region. Their high-quality rubber materials are used in various industries, including railway infrastructure. Our partnership with Kyivguma enables us to utilize reliable and innovative materials to enhance the quality and durability of our projects.
